### **2. Local Civic Alerts**
*   **Heatwave Warning:** The IMD has issued a yellow alert for Bengaluru today. Temperatures are expected to soar to **38°C**. Residents are advised to stay hydrated and avoid outdoor activities during peak afternoon hours (12:00 PM – 4:00 PM).

While a 10km surveillance zone is in place near the site, the Health Department has advised residents city-wide to ensure poultry meat and eggs are thoroughly cooked before consumption. Avoid contact with sick or dead birds.
*   **Water Supply:** No major scheduled maintenance has been announced for the KR Puram/Hoodi wards today, but residents are encouraged to use water judiciously due to the ongoing summer demand.
