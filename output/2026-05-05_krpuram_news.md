### **1. Traffic Update: Tin Factory & Hanging Bridge**
*   **Peak Congestion:** Traffic at **Tin Factory Junction** and the **KR Puram Hanging Bridge** remains heavy this morning. The "long weekend return rush" (following May Day on May 1st) has largely subsided, but the intersection is seeing a typical high-volume Tuesday flow.
*   **Construction Delays:** Ongoing **Namma Metro Phase 2A (Blue Line)** and road-widening work near the KR Puram Railway Station continue to create a "bottleneck" effect. Commuters coming from Hoskote/Old Madras Road towards Mahadevapura should expect a 15–20 minute delay at the bridge entrance.
*   **Advisory:** The **Hebbal Flyover up-ramp** remains restricted due to infrastructure work; commuters heading toward the airport are advised to use alternative routes via Nagavara.

### **2. Local Civic Alerts & Monsoon Readiness**
*   **Flood Mitigation Deadline:** The **Greater Bengaluru Authority (GBA)** has issued a final warning to civic engineers to complete all stormwater drain desilting by **May 30**. 
*   **High-Risk Areas:** Residents in **Geddalahalli**, **Sai Layout**, and low-lying areas near Vengaiah Lake are advised to report any uncleared debris or blocked drains to the Sahaya helpline, as pre-monsoon showers have already caused localized waterlogging earlier this week.
*   **Cleanup Operations:** Work is still underway to clear remnants of tree falls from the severe hailstorm on April 29. Most arterial roads are clear, but interior lanes in KR Puram may still have temporary obstructions.
