**KR PURAM LOCAL BULLETIN: SATURDAY, MARCH 14, 2026**

### **1. Traffic Update: Tin Factory & Hanging Bridge**
*   **Current Status:** Heavy congestion is reported at **Tin Factory Junction** and the **KR Puram Hanging Bridge** due to ongoing Phase 2A/2B Metro works and high weekend exit-traffic toward Hoskote.
*   **Bus Stop Shift:** Reminder for commuters that the bus stop at the mouth of the Hanging Bridge has been shifted **100 meters back** (near the skywalk) to prevent bottlenecks on the bridge ramp.
*   **Route Alert:** The **Lowry Underpass** is operational, but slow-moving. Vehicles heading toward ITPL from the city are advised to use the **Hoodi Main Road** as an alternative to avoid the U-turn chaos at Tin Factory.

### **2. Local Civic Alerts**
*   **Power (BESCOM):** Good news for residents—BESCOM has announced **no scheduled power cuts** for KR Puram today. While maintenance work was conducted earlier this week in the Whitefield division, the grid is expected to remain stable throughout the day.
*   **Water (BWSSB):** A general summer alert is in place. While BWSSB reports sufficient storage in the KRS and Kabini dams, residents in the KR Puram/Ramamurthy Nagar belt are advised to use water judiciously. The **"Blue Force"** is actively monitoring for potable water wastage (fines up to ₹5,000 for car washing or gardening).
