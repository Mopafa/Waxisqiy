# KR Puram Local News Bulletin: March 13, 2026

**Date:** Friday, March 13, 2026  
**Location:** KR Puram, Bengaluru

---

### 🚦 Traffic & Transit Update
*   **Tin Factory & Hanging Bridge:** Expect heavy congestion during peak hours. Construction of the **Metro Blue Line (Airport Line)** between KR Puram and Hebbal continues to impact traffic flow. While the operational Purple Line has eased some pressure, the "chicken-neck" at Tin Factory remains a major bottleneck due to ongoing pillar work and road narrowing.
*   **Commuter Alert:** Commuters traveling toward Marathahalli or Whitefield via the Outer Ring Road should account for an additional 15–20 minutes of travel time. No new major diversions are reported for today, but standard peak-hour crawl is expected at the Kasturi Nagar downramp.

### ⚡ Civic Alerts & Notices
*   **Power Supply:** **BESCOM** has announced **no scheduled power cuts** for the KR Puram division today, March 13. However, localized emergency maintenance may occur.
*   **Civic Governance:** Residents are advised that the **Greater Bengaluru Authority (GBA)** has officially replaced the BBMP structure. The final voter list for the upcoming June 2026 civic polls is set to be published on **March 16**. Residents can check their ward status and electoral details on the official GBA/BBMP portal.
*   **Property Tax:** The GBA is currently conducting a special drive for property tax collection for the FY 2025–26. Defaulters may face penalties as the financial year-end approaches.

### 🎨 Holi 2026 Celebrations
*   **Post-Holi Events:** While official Holi (Rangwali Holi) took place on March 4, some "late Holi" club celebrations are still active in the city. 
    *   **Phoolon Ki Holi:** Ongoing at *Nolimmits Lounge* (near CBD) through March 25.
    *   **Weekend Vibes:** Most major open-air events in KR Puram (like those at *Bella House*) concluded last week. Residents looking for festivities today are encouraged to check local venues in **Indiranagar** or **Whitefield** for indoor themed DJ nights.
*   **Safety Note:** Authorities remind residents to avoid using synthetic colors near water bodies and to remain cautious of "water balloon" activities on public roads, which remain under police surveillance.

**Resident Tip:** Use the **Namma Metro** to bypass the Tin Factory congestion if traveling toward Baiyappanahalli or Whitefield. Safe travels!
