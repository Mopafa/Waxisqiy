#### **2. Local Civic Alerts & Advisories**
*   **Water Conservation (BWSSB):** A city-wide ban on using drinking water for non-essential purposes is currently in effect. This includes **car washing, gardening, construction, and filling swimming pools**. Violators face a fine of **₹5,000**.
*   **Mandatory Aerators:** The BWSSB has set a deadline of **July 31, 2026**, for all apartments and commercial complexes to install flow restrictors (aerators) on taps. Failure to comply may result in a **50% reduction** in water supply.
