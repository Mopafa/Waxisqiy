**KR PURAM LOCAL BULLETIN: JULY 23, 2026**

### **🚦 Traffic Update: Tin Factory & Hanging Bridge**
*   **Current Status:** Expect significant congestion at the **Tin Factory junction** and the **KR Puram Hanging Bridge** throughout the day.
*   **Key Bottlenecks:** Heavy slow-moving traffic is reported on the ramp coming from Ramamurthy Nagar towards the bridge. Vehicles merging from the Outer Ring Road (ORR) and Old Madras Road (OMR) are causing gridlocks near the Tin Factory bus stop.
*   **Advisory:** Redevelopment work on the **Silk Board to KR Puram stretch** is intensifying; motorists are advised to use the Purple Line Metro for travel towards Indiranagar or Whitefield to bypass the junction.
*   **Pedestrian Alert:** The foot-over-bridge connecting the KR Puram Metro to the Railway Station is operational, but police personnel are currently manning the grade-level crossing to manage crowds during peak hours.

### **⚡ Local Civic Alerts**
*   **Power Outages:** **BESCOM** has scheduled maintenance work today (July 23). While the primary focus is on the Bharatiya City and Shobha Indraprastha substations, nearby localities in the KR Puram/Mahadevapura belt may experience intermittent disruptions between **10:00 AM and 4:00 PM**.
*   **Water Supply:** Following the 12-hour Cauvery water disruption on July 22 for emergency valve repairs, supply is expected to stabilize today. However, residents in high-rise apartments may still experience low pressure.
*   **Abandoned Vehicle Drive:** The Greater Bengaluru Authority (GBA) and Traffic Police are continuing their drive to seize abandoned vehicles. If you have a vehicle parked on a public footpath or road for over 7 days, it is liable for seizure and auction.

### **🌸 Holi 2026 Clarification**
*   **Date Check:** Please note that **Holi 2026 was celebrated on March 3rd and 4th, 2026**. There are no official Holi celebrations occurring today, July 23. The next major local festive events will be related to the upcoming monsoon festivals in August.
