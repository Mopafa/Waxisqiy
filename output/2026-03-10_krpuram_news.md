**KR PURAM LOCAL BULLETIN: MARCH 10, 2026**

### **1. Traffic Update: Tin Factory & Hanging Bridge**
*   **Persistent Congestion:** Traffic at the **Tin Factory/MM Temple junction** remains heavy today. Commuters should expect delays of 15–20 minutes during peak hours due to residual bottlenecks from the ongoing Metro Phase 2B work.
*   **Hebbal Flyover Impact:** For those commuting toward the city, the **KR Puram up-ramp of the Hebbal Flyover** remains restricted for ongoing track addition work. Use alternative routes via Lingarajapuram or Nagavara-Tannery Road.
*   **Future Infrastructure:** The state budget (presented March 6) has officially approved a **₹40,000 crore tunnel road** project connecting KR Puram to Mysuru Road to alleviate long-term congestion. A separate ₹450 crore upgrade for the Silk Board to KR Puram ORR corridor is also in the pipeline.

### **2. Local Civic Alerts**
*   **LPG Supply Crisis:** **Major Alert:** Many local hotels and eateries in KR Puram may suspend kitchen operations starting today, **March 10**, due to a sudden halt in commercial LPG cylinder deliveries. Residents are advised to check with local restaurants before visiting.
*   **Water Scarcity Warning:** KR Puram has been identified as one of 65 wards facing **severe groundwater stress**. The BWSSB has launched a ₹10-crore emergency plan, which includes deploying mini-water tanks. Residents are urged to use water judiciously.
*   **Property Tax Enforcement:** The Greater Bengaluru Authority (GBA) has begun **auctioning properties** of major tax defaulters in the KR Puram zone. Over 1,000 final warnings have been issued city-wide.
*   **Weather:** Expect a hot, sunny day with a high of **30°C (86°F)** and 0% chance of rain.

### **3. Holi 2026 Celebrations**
*   **Festival Status:** Please note that the primary Holi celebrations for 2026 took place on **March 3–4**. 
*   **Ongoing Events:** While the main public holiday has passed, some venues in the Mahadevapura/KR Puram belt are hosting extended "Phoolon Ki Holi" (Flower Holi) events. **Nolimmits Lounge** and other local clubs have themed music nights scheduled through the week.
*   **Safety Tip:** Public color-throwing on streets is not expected today; however, increased police patrolling continues in the East Division to monitor post-festival gatherings.

**Resident Advice:** Expect higher-than-usual wait times for food deliveries today due to the LPG shortage. Plan your evening commute early to avoid the "chicken neck" congestion near the KR Puram Railway Station.
