### **KR Puram Local Bulletin: Wednesday, June 17, 2026**

#### **1. Traffic Update: Tin Factory & Hanging Bridge**
*   **Infrastructure Status:** The **Lowry Under Bridge**, which was closed earlier this month for railway slab replacement, is now fully operational. Traffic flow towards ITI Colony, Ramamurthy Nagar, and KR Puram has returned to its normal pattern.
*   **Current Conditions:** Expect typical peak-hour congestion at the **Tin Factory junction** and the **Hanging Bridge**. Commuters are advised to account for additional travel time (15–20 minutes) due to evening rain forecasts which often lead to slow-moving traffic and minor waterlogging in the bottleneck areas under the bridge.

#### **2. Local Civic Alerts**
*   **Weather Warning (Rain Alert):** The IMD has issued a **yellow alert** for Bangalore today. While the morning is expected to be overcast and humid (high of 30°C), there is a **60% chance of thundery showers** in the late afternoon and evening. Residents in low-lying areas of KR Puram should be prepared for potential waterlogging.
*   **Upcoming Strike (June 18):** Over 58,000 small traders, bakery owners, and street vendors across Bangalore have announced an **indefinite strike starting tomorrow**, Thursday, June 18. Expect limited availability of roadside amenities and local condiments shops starting tonight as they prepare for the protest against alleged official harassment.
