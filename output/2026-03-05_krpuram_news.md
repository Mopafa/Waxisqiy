**KR Puram News Bulletin - March 5, 2026**

Good morning, KR Puram residents! Here's your local news update from KR Puram Live.

## Traffic
Expect heavy congestion this morning around the Tin Factory junction, extending towards the KR Puram bridge and Marathahalli Outer Ring Road. An earlier minor breakdown near the railway underpass has been cleared, but residual delays persist. Commuters heading towards Mahadevapura or Hoodi are advised to consider alternate routes or allow for an extra 20-30 minutes of travel time. Local roads around Seegehalli and TC Palya are moving relatively smoothly.

## Weather
Today, March 5th, 2026, KR Puram will experience a warm and sunny day. The maximum temperature is expected to reach 34 degrees Celsius, with a minimum of 22 degrees Celsius overnight. Humidity will be low, around 40-50%. No rainfall is predicted. Please stay hydrated and avoid prolonged exposure to direct sunlight, especially during peak afternoon hours.

## Local Events
**KR Puram Lake Clean-up Drive:** This Saturday, March 8th, the "Friends of KR Puram Lake" community group is organizing a major clean-up drive from 9 AM to 12 PM. Volunteers are encouraged to join at the main entrance near the Children's Park.
**RWA Meeting - Vasanthapura Layout:** The Vasanthapura Layout RWA will hold its monthly general body meeting tonight at 7:00 PM at the Vasanthapura Community Hall to discuss ongoing infrastructure projects and water supply issues.

## Alerts
**Planned Power Outage:** BESCOM has announced a scheduled power outage tomorrow, March 6th, 2026, from 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM for maintenance work. Areas affected include parts of KR Puram Railway Station Road, Ayyappa Nagar, and surrounding layouts. Residents are advised to make necessary arrangements.
**Water Supply Interruption:** BWSSB has notified residents of a temporary reduction in water pressure across Kadugodi Colony and Siddapura village areas today, March 5th, due to emergency pipeline repairs. Normal supply is expected to resume by late evening.

## Community Updates
The new streetlights installed along the main stretch of Old Madras Road, from Bhattarahalli to the Orion Uptown Mall junction, are now fully operational, significantly improving visibility and safety for pedestrians and motorists alike. This project, initiated by the local corporator's office, was completed ahead of schedule. We've also received positive feedback on the new waste segregation initiatives rolled out in Dooravani Nagar, showing increased participation from residents.
