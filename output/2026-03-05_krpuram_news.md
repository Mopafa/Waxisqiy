**KR Puram News Bulletin - March 5, 2026**

**Traffic:**
Heavy morning traffic on Old Madras Road approaching the Tin Factory flyover and Mahadevapura Underpass. Expect slow movement around the KR Puram Railway Station intersection. Outer Ring Road (ORR) from Mahadevapura towards Marathahalli is experiencing usual peak hour congestion. Local roads in Bhattarahalli and Ramamurthy Nagar are also experiencing moderate traffic. Plan your commute accordingly.

**Weather:**
KR Puram will experience clear skies today. Morning temperatures around 21°C, rising to a maximum of 32°C by afternoon. Humidity levels will be moderate. Expect a sunny and pleasant day.
