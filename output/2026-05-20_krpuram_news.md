**Local News Bulletin: KR Puram, Bangalore**  
**Date:** Wednesday, May 20, 2026  

### 🚦 Traffic & Commute Alert
*   **Tin Factory/Hanging Bridge:** Commuters should expect **heavy congestion** today. A major **Outer Ring Road (ORR) redevelopment project** between Silk Board and KR Pura has officially commenced, leading to narrowed lanes and slower movement.
*   **Weather Impact:** The IMD has issued a **yellow alert** for thunderstorms and heavy rain in Bangalore through today. Residents are advised to anticipate sudden waterlogging at the Tin Factory underpass and the KR Puram Hanging Bridge approach roads, which may significantly increase travel time during evening peak hours.
*   **Alternate Routes:** Traffic police recommend using the newly partially opened service lanes near Benniganahalli Metro Station where possible, though the ORR remains the primary bottleneck.

### ⚡ Civic Alerts & Infrastructure
*   **Power Outage:** BESCOM confirmed interruptions today in parts of **KR Puram, B Narayanapura, and Mahadevapura** due to maintenance at the **66/11kV ITI MUSS F15 Feeder**. While restoration was estimated for 2:00 PM, some localized fluctuations may continue as transformer maintenance concludes.
*   **Monsoon Preparedness:** BBMP (Bengaluru East Corporation) is currently executing a **45 km drain desilting drive** in the KR Puram zone. Expect minor roadside obstructions near major storm-water drains as crews clear silt ahead of the heavy June rains.
*   **Water Restrictions:** The BWSSB’s ban on using potable water for non-essential purposes (car washing, gardening, etc.) remains in effect. A fine of **₹5,000** is being strictly enforced for violations.
