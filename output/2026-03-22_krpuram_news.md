**KR Puram Local Bulletin: March 22, 2026**

### 🚦 Traffic Update: Tin Factory & Hanging Bridge
*   **Major Accident Alert:** Traffic on the **Medahalli Flyover** (connecting to the KR Puram Hanging Bridge) is significantly impacted today following a tragic early-morning collision. A goods vehicle rammed into a stationary truck, resulting in three fatalities. Expect heavy residual congestion and slow-moving traffic on **Old Madras Road** towards the city.
*   **Enforcement Drive:** KR Puram Traffic Police have intensified surveillance at **Bhattarahalli and Medahalli junctions** following a recent crackdown where 22 individuals were booked for dangerous wheelie stunts.
*   **Bus Bay Changes:** Commuters at **Tin Factory** are reminded that the bus stop at the mouth of the Hanging Bridge remains shifted 100 meters back (near the skywalk) to prevent the usual bottleneck at the bridge entry.

### ⚡ Civic Alerts & Utilities
*   **Power Supply:** Good news for residents—**BESCOM has confirmed no scheduled power cuts** for any Bengaluru zones today, Sunday, March 22.
*   **Governance Shift:** Under the new **Greater Bengaluru Authority (GBA)** restructuring, the KR Puram assembly constituency has officially been designated as part of the new **Bengaluru East Corporation**. Local administrative services are expected to transition to this new division over the coming weeks.
*   **Water Supply:** No major BWSSB maintenance alerts are active for the KR Puram/Mahadevapura belt today; however, residents are advised to maintain standard storage levels as the city manages seasonal demand.

*   **Date Clarification:** While many residents are inquiring about Holi today, the official **Holi 2026 celebrations took place on March 4**. Today (March 22) marks the post-Holi period; no major public color-throwing events are sanctioned in KR Puram today.
*   **Alternative Events:** 
    *   **Comedy:** For those heading North/East, **Russell Peters** is performing his "RELAX* World Tour" today at **Bhartiya Mall of Bengaluru** (Gates open 4:30 PM).

    *   **Dining:** Post-Ugadi (March 19) food festivals continue at major malls in the Whitefield/KR Puram area, offering traditional Karnataka festive menus.

**Resident Advice:** Avoid the Medahalli-Hanging Bridge stretch if possible until noon to allow accident investigation and vehicle clearance to conclude.
