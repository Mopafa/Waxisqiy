This factual bulletin summarizes the local news, traffic conditions, and civic updates for KR Puram, Bangalore, for today, **Sunday, March 15, 2026**.

### **1. Traffic Update: Tin Factory & Hanging Bridge**
*   **Routine Congestion:** Traffic at the Tin Factory junction and the KR Puram Hanging Bridge remains slow-moving, as is typical for Sunday afternoons. Heavy interstate bus movement is expected towards the evening at the Tin Factory boarding points.
*   **CBD Impact:** Residents traveling toward the city center should avoid **M.G. Road**. The 15-day asphalting project that began on March 5 is entering its final stages; traffic is being diverted to alternative routes, causing significant snarls in the Central Business District (CBD).

### **2. Local Civic Alerts**
*   **Water Shortage Warning:** An Indian Institute of Science (IISc) report and a BWSSB advisory have placed **KR Puram** among 65 wards predicted to face acute water scarcity this summer. Residents are advised to store water and use it judiciously as groundwater levels in the East zone continue to deplete.
*   **Property Tax Auctions:** The Greater Bengaluru Authority (GBA) has issued final warnings to major tax defaulters in the **KR Pura zone**. Six properties were recently identified for auction due to long-standing dues; residents are urged to clear any outstanding property taxes to avoid enforcement action.
*   **Waste Management:** A new garbage transfer system is being implemented. Plans are underway to establish a high-capacity transfer station in the KR Puram stretch to eliminate roadside waste loading, which is expected to reduce local hygiene complaints and traffic disruptions in the long term.
*   **Civic Restructuring:** Under the Greater Bengaluru Governance Act, the BBMP has officially transitioned into five separate entities. KR Puram now falls under the **Bengaluru East Corporation**, which will now manage local administrative affairs.
