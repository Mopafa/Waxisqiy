**KR Puram Local Bulletin: Tuesday, April 28, 2026**

### **1. Traffic & Transit: Tin Factory / Hanging Bridge**
*   **Current Status:** Heavy congestion continues at the Tin Factory junction and Hanging Bridge during peak hours (8:30 AM – 10:30 AM). Average speeds in the area are reported at **10–12 km/h**.

*   **Route Alert:** Reminder for commuters that the stretch from BBMP Junction to the Veterinary Hospital (near KR Puram Police Station) remains **one-way**. Vehicles from Anandapura/KR Puram village heading to the city must use Old Madras Road and U-turn near GRT.

### **2. Local Civic Alerts**
*   **Heatwave Warning:** The Bengaluru District Disaster Management Authority has issued an alert as temperatures hover around **35°C**. Residents are advised to stay hydrated and avoid outdoor activities between **12:00 PM and 3:00 PM**.
*   **Power Supply:** No major scheduled maintenance cuts are listed for KR Puram today. However, residents in KR Puram II Stage and HRBR have reported intermittent **load shedding** (approx. 40-minute intervals) following a transformer trip at the Banaswadi Station yesterday.
*   **Security News:** Local police are currently investigating an incident in **Muniyappa Layout (KR Pura II Stage)** where a tender coconut seller allegedly attacked a street dog. An FIR has been registered under the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals Act.

### **3. Holi 2026 Celebrations**
