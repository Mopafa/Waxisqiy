### **KR Puram Local News Bulletin: July 6, 2026**

**Current Date:** Monday, July 6, 2026  
**Status:** Operational Update for KR Puram Residents

---

#### **1. Traffic Update: Tin Factory & Hanging Bridge**
*   **Peak Hour Gridlock:** As of 9:00 AM, heavy congestion is reported at the **Tin Factory junction** and the base of the **KR Puram Hanging Bridge** (Byappanahalli side). Commuters coming from Old Madras Road (OMR) and the Outer Ring Road (ORR) are experiencing delays of 20–30 minutes due to the convergence of traffic.
*   **Bottleneck Alert:** Vehicles merging near the KR Puram Railway Station are facing significant slowdowns. Residents are advised to avoid the bridge ramp if heading toward ITPL; use the lower service roads where possible, though these are also seeing high volume.
*   **Public Transit:** BMTC buses are currently bunching at the entry of the flyover, further narrowing available lanes. No major accidents have been reported this morning.

#### **2. Civic Alerts & Infrastructure**
*   **Water Usage Restrictions (City-wide):** The **BWSSB** has issued a strict directive effective today. Potable (drinking) water is **banned** for washing vehicles, cleaning roads, and filling swimming pools. A fine of **₹5,000** will be imposed for violations. Additionally, the installation of water aerators is now mandatory for all residential and commercial buildings in KR Puram.
*   **Power Supply:** While no major scheduled outages are listed specifically for KR Puram today, neighboring **Kodugodi** and **Kodigehalli** are experiencing a planned shutdown from **9:00 AM to 12:00 PM** for maintenance.
*   **Footpath Clearance:** BBMP continues its "footpath clearance drive" along the main OMR corridor to improve pedestrian safety. Avoid parking two-wheelers on pavements near the KR Puram market area to avoid towing.

#### **3. Holi 2026 Status Report**
*   **Fact Check:** There are **no Holi celebrations** happening today. 
*   **Calendar Correction:** For 2026, the festival of Holi (Rangwali Holi) was celebrated on **March 4, 2026**. Today, July 6, is a standard working Monday. Residents may be seeing social media "throwbacks" or late-season resort events, but no official or traditional Holi festivities are scheduled for this date.

#### **4. Local Weather & Safety**
*   **IMD Alert:** An "Orange Alert" remains in place for coastal regions, which may bring sporadic, heavy rain showers to Bengaluru by the evening.
*   **Safety Note:** Following a recent incident in KR Puram involving a school van, local traffic police have increased inspections of private transport vehicles. Ensure all school-going children's transport providers are adhering to safety lock protocols.
