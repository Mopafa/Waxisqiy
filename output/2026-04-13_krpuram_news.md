### **KR Puram Local News Bulletin: April 13, 2026**

#### **1. Festival Alert: Holi 2026 vs. Baisakhi**
*   **Holi Update:** Residents are advised that **Holi 2026 has already concluded** (it was celebrated on March 3–4, 2026). 
*   **Today’s Celebration:** Today, April 13, marks the celebration of **Baisakhi**, the harvest festival. Special prayers and *langars* are expected at Gurudwaras across Bangalore, particularly in areas with a significant Punjabi community. Regional New Year festivals like **Vishu** (Kerala) and **Puthandu** (Tamil Nadu) will be observed tomorrow, April 14.

#### **2. Traffic Update: Tin Factory & Hanging Bridge**
*   **Tin Factory Junction:** Traffic is moving at its typical slow pace. While the recent widening of roads and the introduction of new bus bays have slightly reduced the bottleneck, commuters should still expect a **15–20 minute delay** during peak evening hours (5:00 PM – 8:00 PM).
*   **Hanging Bridge:** Movement remains steady but heavy for vehicles coming from Mahadevapura towards Hebbal. 
*   **Peenya Flyover Alert:** For those traveling toward North Bangalore, the previously scheduled load testing and closure of the **Peenya Flyover (April 13–17)** has been **postponed**. Vehicle movement is currently continuing as usual, according to traffic authorities.

#### **3. Local Civic Alerts & Utilities**
*   **Power Supply:** BESCOM has scheduled maintenance in several zones today. While major parts of KR Puram are not on the primary outage list, neighboring areas in the **East Zone** (like Ramamurthy Nagar and parts of Marathahalli) may experience intermittent disruptions between **10:00 AM and 5:00 PM**.
*   **Water Supply:** No major BWSSB shutdowns are reported for the KR Puram/Mahadevapura zone today. However, residents are encouraged to store water as a precaution due to ongoing Cauvery Stage V pipeline auxiliary works in the wider East Bangalore region.

#### **4. Local Crime & Safety**
*   **Police Action:** Local authorities recently apprehended a duo in KR Puram for a high-profile blackmail case involving a married woman. Residents are urged to be cautious regarding digital privacy and report suspicious activity to the KR Puram Police Station.
*   **General Safety:** Following recent incidents near the KR Puram Railway Station, police patrols have been increased in the deserted spots around the station and the Hanging Bridge underpass during late-night hours.

**Resident Advice:** Plan your commute using the Purple Line Metro to bypass the Tin Factory junction. If traveling toward the airport or Hebbal, monitor live maps for sudden congestion near the KR Puram down-ramp.
