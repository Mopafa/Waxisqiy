**KR Puram Local Bulletin: Monday, April 27, 2026**

### **1. Traffic Update: Tin Factory & Hanging Bridge**
*   **Status:** Moderate to heavy congestion reported during peak hours. While the **Purple Line Metro** has reduced overall vehicle queue lengths at the Tin Factory junction by nearly 50% since its full operational launch, the bottleneck remains a "slow-moving zone" due to ongoing infrastructure work for the **Blue Line (Phase 2A)** towards the Airport.
*   **Tin Factory Junction:** Expect delays near the MM Temple side. Traffic police are strictly enforcing the "No-Halt" rule for private buses at the mouth of the Hanging Bridge to prevent pile-ups towards Kasturi Nagar.
*   **Hanging Bridge:** Commuters heading towards Hebbal and ITPL should watch for potholes on the down-ramp, which have been aggravated by recent pre-monsoon showers.
*   **City-Wide Relief:** The **Central Silk Board double-decker flyover** is now fully operational as of today, significantly easing the commute for those traveling from KR Puram towards HSR Layout and Electronic City via the Outer Ring Road (ORR).

### **2. Local Civic Alerts**
*   **Water Supply:** The **BWSSB** has confirmed that the 24-hour supply disruption for the installation of EMF meters (Cauvery 5th Stage) ended at 6:00 AM this morning. Residents in **Hoodi, Whitefield, and KR Puram** may experience low pressure until the pipelines fully recharge by late evening.
*   **Power Supply:** No major scheduled power cuts are announced by **BESCOM** for KR Puram today. However, routine localized maintenance may occur in sections of **Seegehalli** and **Ramamurthy Nagar** between 11:00 AM and 3:00 PM.
*   **BBMP Notice:** Anti-encroachment drives continue in the Mahadevapura zone. Residents are advised to ensure no construction materials block the secondary stormwater drains (SWDs) to avoid penalties.

### **3. Festival Alert: Holi & Thrissur Pooram**
*   **Holi 2026:** Note that the official **Holi (Rangwali)** was celebrated on **March 4, 2026**. There are no major city-wide Holi celebrations scheduled for today.
*   **Thrissur Pooram (April 27):** While not Holi, the iconic **Thrissur Pooram** (Kerala’s grandest temple festival) is being celebrated today. Given the large Malayali population in East Bangalore, expect local cultural processions and "Pooram" themed events in KR Puram and nearby Marathahalli.
*   **Other Events:** 
    *   **Performing Arts:** A Kuchipudi dance performance titled *"Dāri | The Path"* is happening at the **Bangalore International Centre (BIC)** from 7:00 PM.
    *   **Food:** The *MTR Karunadu Swada* food festival continues at Jayamahal Palace, featuring authentic regional cuisines of Karnataka.

**Resident Advice:** Plan for an extra 15 minutes of travel time if crossing Tin Factory between 5:30 PM and 8:00 PM. Ensure overhead tanks are filled by noon as water pressure stabilizes.
