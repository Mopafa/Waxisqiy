**KR Puram Local News Bulletin – Thursday, July 16, 2026**

### **1. Major Civic Alert: 12-Hour Water Supply Disruption**
Residents of KR Puram and surrounding areas should prepare for a significant water outage today.
*   **Time:** 6:00 AM to 6:00 PM (12 hours).
*   **Reason:** Emergency maintenance by the BWSSB to fix a leakage in the 2700 mm diameter pipeline of the Cauvery Water Supply Stage IV, Phase II.
*   **Impacted Localities:** Part of **ITI Layout (KR Puram)**, along with nearby areas such as Sanjay Nagar, Amruthahalli, and HSR Layout. Residents are advised to store sufficient water for daily needs.

### **2. Traffic Update: Tin Factory & Hanging Bridge**
Commuters should expect the usual heavy congestion at the KR Puram bottlenecks.
*   **Tin Factory Junction:** Movement remains slow due to ongoing Namma Metro Blue Line construction. Traffic moving toward Hebbal and Indiranagar is seeing peak-hour delays of 20–30 minutes.
*   **Hanging Bridge:** Minor bottlenecks reported at the mouth of the bridge (HP fuel station side) due to crisscrossing traffic from the Outer Ring Road (ORR) and Old Madras Road (OMR).
*   **Road Safety Note:** Authorities have increased patrolling on the Tin Factory flyover following a recent fatal accident involving a minor in June. Motorists are urged to strictly follow lane discipline.
