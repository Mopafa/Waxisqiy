### **KR Puram Local News Bulletin: March 26, 2026**

#### **1. Traffic & Transit Alert**
*   **Marathahalli Bridge Closure (Upcoming):** The Bengaluru Traffic Police has announced a **three-day total closure** of the Marathahalli Bridge starting tomorrow, **Friday, March 27, through Sunday, March 29**. This is to facilitate critical Metro Phase 2A (Blue Line) construction. Residents heading toward HAL or Whitefield should prepare for heavy diversions and significant spillover traffic today as pre-closure work begins.
*   **Tin Factory/Hanging Bridge:** High congestion remains at the Tin Factory junction due to ongoing metro work for the Blue and Purple lines. Traffic flow is being regulated by automated signals at the mouth of the Hanging Bridge. Commuters are reminded to use the designated bus bays below the skywalk (100m from the bridge mouth) as stopping on the bridge is strictly prohibited.
*   **Local Congestion:** Expect localized slow-moving traffic around the KR Puram Railway Station and the TC Palya junction during peak evening hours (5:00 PM – 8:30 PM).

#### **2. Festive Celebrations: Rama Navami & Holi**
*   **Rama Navami (Today):** While the user inquired about Holi, today, **March 26, 2026, is actually Rama Navami**. Expect increased pedestrian activity near the Sri Rama Temple areas.

#### **3. Local Civic Alerts**
*   **Civic Budgets:** Today marks the final day of the first-ever independent budget presentations for the five new corporations under the **Greater Bengaluru Authority (GBA)**. Residents of KR Puram (Mahadevapura zone) should stay tuned for local infrastructure funding updates following the East Corporation's budget release.
