### **KR Puram Local News Bulletin: June 18, 2026**

**1. Traffic & Transit Alert (Tin Factory / Hanging Bridge)**
*   **Major Accident Update:** A tragic accident occurred this morning on the **Tin Factory flyover**. A minor motorcyclist reportedly lost control and hit a divider, resulting in a fatality. This has caused significant congestion and slow-moving traffic on the **Outer Ring Road (ORR) bypass** and the approach towards the Hanging Bridge. 
*   **General Traffic:** Commuters are advised to expect heavy tailbacks extending toward Benniganahalli and Mahadevapura. The **Hanging Bridge** remains a bottleneck; use the Lowry Under Bridge as an alternative if heading toward Ramamurthy Nagar, as recent maintenance there was completed on June 9.

**2. Local Civic Alerts & Updates**
*   **Traders’ Strike (Citywide Bandh):** Over 58,000 small traders, including bakery owners, street vendors, and condiment shopkeepers in Bengaluru, have announced an indefinite strike starting today, **June 18**. Residents in KR Puram may find many local bakeries and small retail shops closed today in protest against alleged government harassment.
*   **Power Supply:** No major scheduled power cuts are reported specifically for KR Puram today by BESCOM. However, maintenance is ongoing in nearby **HRBR Layout** (10:00 AM – 1:00 PM). Unscheduled outages remain possible due to pre-monsoon grid stress.
*   **Water Supply:** BWSSB has identified KR Puram as a **high-alert water-stressed zone** for the current season. While supply is stable today, a major **24-hour citywide water shutdown** is scheduled for tomorrow, **June 19**, to facilitate Cauvery Stage V pipeline integration. Residents are advised to store water in advance.

**3. Holi 2026 Celebrations**
*   **Correction:** Please note that **Holi 2026 was celebrated on March 4, 2026**. There are no official Holi festivities or "Rain Holi" events scheduled for today, June 18. Local community calendars show no major cultural events for this date, though some private clubs may host monsoon-themed gatherings unrelated to the festival of colors.

**Resident Advice:**
*   Avoid the Tin Factory flyover until the accident site is fully cleared.
*   Stock up on daily essentials early, as the traders' strike may limit the availability of fresh bakery items and street-side goods.
*   Begin water conservation measures ahead of tomorrow's scheduled 24-hour cutoff.
