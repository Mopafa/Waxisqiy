This is your local news bulletin for **KR Puram, Bangalore**, for **Monday, March 9, 2026**.

### **1. Traffic & Transit: Tin Factory / Hanging Bridge**
*   **Congestion Status:** Heavy traffic persists at the **Tin Factory junction** and the **KR Puram Hanging Bridge** due to ongoing Namma Metro Blue Line construction (KR Puram to Airport). 
*   **Operational Changes:** To mitigate the "bottleneck" effect, traffic police continue to enforce the 100-meter shift of the bus stop away from the mouth of the Hanging Bridge. Commuters should use the designated bay near the Benniganahalli Metro Station.
*   **Local Disruptions:** A **"Woman-athon"** held this morning in nearby Kalyan Nagar (to celebrate International Women’s Day) may have caused residual delays on roads connecting Kalyan Nagar to the Outer Ring Road.
*   **Ongoing Alerts:** **Nagawara Main Road** remains closed until March 22 for white-topping work; vehicles are being diverted through parallel residential lanes.

### **2. Civic Alerts & Maintenance**
*   **Night Garbage Collection:** Starting today, **BBMP** is expanding its **night-time garbage collection** program to KR Puram and surrounding blocks. Residents are advised to segregate waste and follow the new night-time pickup schedules to prevent roadside dumping.
*   **Power Supply:** While major maintenance occurred over the weekend (March 7–8), **BESCOM** has reported minor intermittent disruptions in the Eastern Zone today for feeder stabilization. No long-duration outages are officially scheduled for KR Puram today, but neighboring areas like Kengeri are facing 10 am–5 pm cuts.
*   **Infrastructure Update:** Following the **Karnataka Budget 2026** presented last Friday, preliminary surveys for the proposed **40km tunnel road** (connecting KR Puram to Mysuru Road) are expected to begin soon.

### **3. Holi 2026 Celebrations**
*   **Festival Status:** The main **Rangwali Holi** was celebrated last Wednesday, March 4. 
*   **Events Today:** Most open-air rain dances have concluded. However, the **"Holi @Gokarna"** trek groups are returning to the city today. For those still looking to celebrate, **Nolimmits Lounge** is hosting an extended "Phoolon Ki Holi" (Flower Holi) event that continues through the month.
*   **Safety Note:** Authorities have issued a wrap-up advisory to avoid using strong synthetic dyes in residential areas as cleanup efforts by the BWSSB continue amidst the seasonal water scarcity.
