This bulletin provides a factual update for residents of **KR Puram, Bangalore**, for **Tuesday, April 14, 2026**.

### **1. Local Civic Alerts & Public Holiday**
*   **Ambedkar Jayanti:** Today is a gazetted public holiday in honor of Dr. B.R. Ambedkar. Educational institutions (schools and colleges), government offices, and many private sectors are **closed**.
*   **Utility & Services:** Bank branches are closed for public transactions. Emergency services and hospitals remain operational.
*   **Weather Warning:** Temperatures have risen significantly, with local reports indicating mercury levels breaching **36°C**. Residents are advised to stay hydrated and avoid direct sun exposure between 12:00 PM and 4:00 PM.

### **2. Traffic Update (Tin Factory & Hanging Bridge)**
*   **Current Status:** Traffic flow is **moderate** and significantly lighter than a typical Tuesday morning due to the public holiday.
*   **Bottlenecks:** 
    *   **Tin Factory:** Standard slow movement persists at the junction where BMTC buses merge. The shift of the bus stop (100m behind the skywalk) continues to help, but avoid the "mouth" of the hanging bridge where minor idling is reported.
    *   **Hanging Bridge:** Movement is smooth across the cable-stayed bridge. However, the descent toward KR Puram Railway Station remains a bottleneck due to local market activity.
*   **Ongoing Disruptions:** Be aware of slow-moving traffic on the Outer Ring Road (ORR) near the **Salem Railway Bridge** due to residual metro/composite girder work. Commuters heading toward Marathahalli from KR Puram may experience 10–15 minute delays in this section.

### **3. Holi 2026 Celebrations**
*   **Important Correction:** Holi 2026 was officially celebrated on **Wednesday, March 4, 2026**. There are **no Holi color-play events happening today.**
*   **Today's Festivals:** While it is not Holi, today marks several regional New Year celebrations, including **Puthandu (Tamil New Year)** and **Vishu**. You may notice increased footfall near local temples, particularly the Mahabaleshwara and Kote Venkataramana temples in the vicinity.
*   **Past Events:** Major KR Puram Holi parties (like the Open Air Rain Dance at Bella House) took place in early March.

**Resident Tip:** Since it is a holiday, expect increased crowds at **Phoenix Marketcity** and **VR Bengaluru** malls later this evening. Traffic on ITPL Main Road is likely to spike after 5:00 PM.
