**KR Puram Local Bulletin: June 22, 2026**

### **1. Traffic & Transit Updates**
*   **Tin Factory/Hanging Bridge:** Heavy congestion is reported during peak morning and evening hours. Commuters should expect the standard 15–20 minute delay at the Tin Factory bottleneck. While the major road closures for the Lowry Road Underbridge (RUB) concluded earlier this month (June 9), traffic remains slow due to ongoing metro-related work and a recent increase in police patrolling following a fatal flyover accident on June 18.
*   **Wider City Impact:** Expect additional slow-moving traffic on routes leading toward the city center (Vidhana Soudha) today due to the **"Bairamangala Chalo"** farmer protest rally.

### **2. Civic Alerts: Power & Water**
*   **Scheduled Power Outages:** BESCOM and KPTCL have announced planned power cuts today, June 22, and tomorrow, June 23, for essential maintenance.
    *   **Affected Areas:** East Bengaluru localities including **Govindapura, Brindavan Nagar, Hegde Nagar**, and parts of the **Outer Ring Road (ORR)** corridor.
    *   **Timings:** Supply is expected to be interrupted between **10:00 AM and 5:00 PM**.
*   **Water Supply:** No specific alerts have been issued for today. However, following the 24-hour Cauvery water disruption on June 19-20, residents are advised to check local storage levels as pressure may still be stabilizing in some tail-end areas of KR Puram.

### **3. Holi 2026 Celebrations**

Residents looking for "Holi" festivities today may be referring to private themed parties, as no public or religious events are listed for this date.

*   **Travel:** Avoid Tin Factory between 5:30 PM and 8:00 PM if possible.
