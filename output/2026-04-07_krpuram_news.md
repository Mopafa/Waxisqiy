### **KR Puram Local News Bulletin: Tuesday, April 7, 2026**

#### **1. Traffic Update: Tin Factory & Hanging Bridge**
*   **Current Status:** Expect standard peak-hour congestion at the **Tin Factory junction** and the **KR Puram Hanging Bridge**. While no emergency traffic blocks have been reported this morning, heavy slow-moving traffic persists due to ongoing **Metro Phase-2A (Blue Line)** construction along the Outer Ring Road.
*   **Commuter Tip:** The automated signal at the mouth of the hanging bridge (towards HP petrol pump) remains operational to manage cross-traffic from Mahadevapura. Commuters heading towards Whitefield or Hebbal are advised to use the **Kasturi Nagar down-ramp** with caution as bus lane violations continue to cause bottlenecks.

#### **2. Local Civic Alerts**
*   **Property Tax Enforcement:** The **Bengaluru East City Corporation** (under the newly formed Greater Bengaluru Authority) has issued a final notice to **21 properties in the KR Puram zone** for unpaid tax dues. Owners have until the end of this month to clear arrears, or the civic body will initiate the process to take over these properties.
*   **Summer Water Supply:** **BWSSB** has placed KR Puram on a "high-alert" list for water-stressed micro-pockets this summer. While Cauvery Stage V has improved overall supply, a **₹10-crore preparedness plan** is in effect. Residents are urged to install aerators in taps and avoid using potable water for vehicle washing or gardening to escape penalties.
*   **Power Supply:** **BESCOM** has confirmed **no scheduled power cuts** for the KR Puram division today. For any localized transformer faults, residents can call 1912.

#### **3. Local News Spotlight**
*   **"Banner Battle" in Vignananagar:** A local controversy has erupted in the **Vignananagar ward** of KR Puram. Around 40 defamatory banners were reportedly put up by miscreants targeting a woman whistle-blower who highlighted illegal encroachments and flooding issues. An FIR has been registered at the HAL Police Station, and an investigation is ongoing.
*   **Safety Alert:** Local police have increased patrolling on **TC Palya Main Road** following a fatal clash late last month involving a resident. Authorities are monitoring high-density areas for any public order disturbances related to local civic activism.

#### **4. Holi 2026 Update**

There are no major Holi celebrations scheduled for today (April 7), as the festival of colors concluded over a month ago. Some small-scale local community gatherings for *Vasanthotsava* may occur, but no public Holi events are active.
