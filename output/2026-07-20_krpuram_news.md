**KR Puram Local News Bulletin: July 20, 2026**

### **1. Traffic Update: Tin Factory & Hanging Bridge**
*   **Current Status:** Routine heavy congestion is reported at the Tin Factory junction and the K.R. Puram Hanging Bridge during peak morning and evening hours. 
*   **Ongoing Work:** Commuters should remain aware of the **Lowry Under Bridge (K.R. Puram)** repair work initiated earlier this season. While major diversions have stabilized, the reduction in lane capacity continues to cause "gridlock" at the base of the flyover on the Baiyappanahalli side.
*   **Advisory:** The Blue Line Metro construction (Phase 2B) continues to impact service roads near the KR Puram Metro station. Pedestrians are advised to use the skywalks as traffic police have intensified enforcement against jaywalking in this high-risk zone.

### **2. Local Civic Alerts**
*   **Abandoned Vehicle Crackdown:** The Greater Bengaluru Authority (GBA) is currently conducting a citywide drive to clear abandoned vehicles from public spaces and footpaths. Residents in K.R. Puram are encouraged to report any long-term stationary vehicles to local ward officials to avoid towing.
*   **Drainage & Road Maintenance:** Following recent ministerial inspections, BBMP officials have been directed to prioritize the clearing of silt from drains in the Mahadevapura zone to prevent waterlogging at underpasses.
*   **Safety Notice:** Beware of viral "lockdown" rumors circulating on social media. There are **no official lockdowns** or major movement restrictions in Bengaluru today; current news confirms these are misdated posts from 2020.

### **3. Holi 2026 Update**
*   **Celebration Status:** There are **no Holi celebrations occurring today**. Holi 2026 was officially celebrated on **March 3rd and 4th, 2026**. 
*   **Upcoming Festivals:** Residents looking for local festivities should note that the **Jagannath Rath Yatra** occurred last week (July 16), and **Ashadhi Ekadashi** celebrations are expected later this week on July 25.

### **4. Major Local News**
*   **Triple Murder Case:** Developments continue in the investigation of the K.R. Puram triple murder case from June. The primary accused, Kenneth, is expected to undergo court-ordered brain mapping tests this week as police finalize the chargesheet.
