### **2. Civic Alerts: Water & Power**
*   **Major Water Disruption:** The **BWSSB** has issued a city-wide alert for today, April 23. A technical fault (electrical flashover due to high heat) at the **Tataguni 220 kVA power station** has disrupted the pumping of Cauvery water. Residents in KR Puram, Ramamurthy Nagar, and Mahadevapura may experience low pressure or a total cutoff. BWSSB expects supply to stabilize by late tonight or tomorrow morning.
*   **Power Supply:** **BESCOM** has not scheduled any major maintenance power cuts for the KR Puram division today. However, due to "near-record heat" impacting the local grid, brief unscheduled outages for load management may occur.
*   **Heat Wave:** Temperatures are expected to touch **37-38°C** today. Residents are advised to stay hydrated and avoid outdoor activities between 12:00 PM and 4:00 PM.
