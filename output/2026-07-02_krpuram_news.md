**KR PURAM LOCAL NEWS BULLETIN: JULY 2, 2026**

### **1. Traffic Update: Tin Factory & Hanging Bridge**
*   **New Metro Feeder Service:** Effective yesterday (July 1), BMTC has introduced a new non-AC Metro feeder route (**MF-22ET**) connecting **Electronic City to Tin Factory**. Buses run from 6:10 AM to 8:25 PM via Marathahalli Bridge and Kadubeesanahalli, providing a vital alternative for commuters.
*   **Current Status:** Traffic at the Tin Factory junction and Hanging Bridge remains heavy but follows typical peak-hour patterns. No emergency road closures are reported today. 
*   **Safety Note:** Authorities have increased vigilance on the Tin Factory flyover following a recent tragic accident involving a minor; commuters are urged to adhere to speed limits and lane discipline.

### **2. Civic Alerts & Public Works**
*   **Power Outages:** While KR Puram is not on today's primary shutdown list, neighboring **HBR Layout** is experiencing a scheduled outage from **11:00 AM to 1:00 PM**. Residents should be prepared for minor fluctuations as BESCOM carries out routine monsoon maintenance in the East Zone.
*   **Water Supply:** BWSSB confirms that water supply remains stable today. Despite a low reservoir level at the Shiva Anaicut, officials have increased releases from the KRS dam to ensure uninterrupted drinking water for Bangalore for the next 4–5 months.
*   **Monsoon Alert:** KR Puram and Mahadevapura have been identified as "monsoon hotspots" by the BBMP. Residents in low-lying areas are advised to stay alert for potential waterlogging if heavy evening showers occur.
