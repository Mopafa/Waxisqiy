**KR Puram Local Bulletin: Wednesday, March 25, 2026**

Here is your concise daily update on traffic, civic alerts, and local events for the KR Puram and Tin Factory area.

### **1. Traffic & Commute Update**
*   **Tin Factory/Hanging Bridge:** Heavy congestion is reported at the mouth of the KR Puram Hanging Bridge. Commuters are reminded that the bus stop has been shifted **100 meters back** (just below the skywalk) to prevent blocking the bridge entrance.
*   **Medahalli Flyover Alert:** Following the tragic accident on March 22 involving a stationary vehicle, traffic police are strictly enforcing "No Parking" zones on flyovers. Expect increased patrolling between Medahalli and KR Puram.
*   **City-Wide Ripple Effects:** While the major protests are centered around **Freedom Park** today (the final day of the March 23–25 demonstrations), expect heavier-than-usual traffic on Old Madras Road as commuters seek alternative routes to avoid the central business district.

### **2. Civic Alerts & Infrastructure**
*   **East Corporation Budget:** Today is a significant day for local governance. The **East Corporation** (under the newly formed Greater Bengaluru Authority) is presenting its first-ever independent budget this morning. Residents should watch for updates regarding local road repairs and park maintenance funds.
*   **Power Supply:** Good news for residents—BESCOM has confirmed that the scheduled maintenance outages from earlier this week have concluded. **No power cuts** are officially scheduled for the KR Puram zone today.
*   **Water Scarcity Preparedness:** BWSSB has listed parts of KR Puram and Mahadevapura as "micro-pockets" for water stress this summer. Sintex buffer tanks (5,000L) are being deployed in interior stretches to manage potential groundwater depletion.

### **3. Holi & Cultural Events**
*   **Holi 2026 Status:** Note that the main Rangwali Holi festival for 2026 took place on **March 4**. However, some local communities in KR Puram are observing **Rang Panchami** festivities today with small-scale gatherings.
*   **Bengaluru Habba (March 25–26):** The city's official "Bengaluru Habba" kicks off today. While the main stage is near Vidhana Soudha and Cubbon Park, local cultural performances and food stalls are expected at major malls near KR Puram, such as Phoenix Marketcity and VR Bengaluru.
*   **Major Concert:** Rock legends **Def Leppard** begin their Bengaluru tour leg today. Expect increased traffic toward the venue (North/Central Bengaluru) starting from 4:00 PM.

**Resident Tip:** If you are heading toward the city center, use the **Purple Line Metro** from KR Puram Station to bypass the protest-related congestion near Freedom Park.
