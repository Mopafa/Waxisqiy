This bulletin provides a concise update for KR Puram residents for **Monday, May 4, 2026**.

### 🚦 Traffic & Infrastructure Update
*   **Tin Factory/Hanging Bridge:** Heavy congestion is reported this morning following intense overnight rains. Expect significant delays at the Tin Factory junction and the approach to the KR Puram Hanging Bridge due to waterlogging in low-lying service roads.
*   **ORR White-Topping Project:** A major civic alert has been issued as **Bengaluru Smart Infrastructure Limited (B-SMILE)** begins the white-topping of the Outer Ring Road (ORR) main carriageway between **Silk Board Junction and KR Puram**. Traffic is expected to operate at half-capacity on this 17.1 km stretch for the next several months, leading to severe bottlenecks.
*   **Metro Work:** Ongoing construction of the **Blue Line (Phase 2A)** continues to impact the median space. Commuters are advised to use the Purple Line metro to avoid the surface-level gridlock where possible.

### ⚠️ Local Civic Alerts
*   **Weather Warning:** The IMD has issued a **Yellow Alert** for KR Puram and eastern Bengaluru today. After heavy showers early this morning (reported in TC Palya and Seegehalli), more thunderstorms and gusty winds (up to 40 kmph) are forecast for the evening.
*   **Rain-Related Hazards:** Residents are urged to avoid the **KR Circle underpass** and other prone areas, as several underpasses in the city have experienced flooding. There are reports of tree falls and snapped branches in the Mahadevapura zone; contact the BBMP helpline (1533) to report obstructions.
*   **Power/Water:** While no scheduled power cuts were announced, localized outages are possible due to the overnight storm. Ensure all drainage inlets near your property are clear to prevent localized flooding during the evening rains.

### 🌸 Holi 2026 Clarification
*   **Date Check:** Please note that **Holi 2026 was celebrated on March 4, 2026**. There are no Holi celebrations scheduled for today, May 4.
*   **Upcoming Events:** Most major festive events for the summer season have concluded; however, local community centers may have indoor monsoon-themed cultural gatherings.

**Resident Advice:** Plan for an additional 30–45 minutes of travel time if using the Outer Ring Road. Keep umbrellas handy for the expected evening downpour.
