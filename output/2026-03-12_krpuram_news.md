**KR PURAM LOCAL BULLETIN: MARCH 12, 2026**

### **1. Traffic Update: Tin Factory & Hanging Bridge**
*   **Current Status:** Expect routine heavy congestion at the **Tin Factory junction** and the **KR Puram Hanging Bridge**. While the recent relocation of the bus stop (shifted 100m away from the bridge mouth towards the skywalk) has slightly improved the "bottleneck" effect, peak-hour delays remain significant.
*   **Ongoing Work:** Metro construction near the Benniganahalli station continues to restrict lane width. Commuters heading towards Whitefield or Marathahalli should account for an additional 20–30 minutes during morning and evening peaks.
*   **Advice:** Use the newly opened skywalk for safer pedestrian crossing and avoid boarding buses at the old bridge entry point to prevent further traffic pile-ups.

### **2. Civic Alerts: Critical Shortages**
*   **LPG Shortage Emergency:** A major city-wide commercial LPG shortage is impacting KR Puram today. Reports indicate that several **PGs (Paying Guest accommodations) in the area have been advised to temporarily halt the preparation of "hot foods"** such as dosas and chapathis to conserve fuel. Residents are advised to check with local tiffin centers, as many have declared a "no-service" day.
*   **Water Crisis (Red Zone):** KR Puram has been identified as a **"High-Risk" locality** for borewell failure in the 2026 Summer Water Alert. The BWSSB’s ₹10.1-crore emergency plan is active. Residents are urged to install aerators on all taps and strictly limit non-essential water usage (car washing, gardening) to avoid fines.
*   **Power Supply:** No major scheduled maintenance is listed for today, but local disruptions may occur due to infrastructure upgrades associated with the ongoing Metro and flyover projects.

### **3. Holi 2026 Celebrations**
*   **Event Status:** The main Holi 2026 festivities occurred earlier this month, on **March 3rd and 4th**. 
*   **Today’s Activity:** There are **no major public Holi celebrations scheduled for today (March 12)**. Large-scale events at venues like *Gold Rush Brews* and *Bella House* in KR Puram concluded during the first week of March. Residents may still see minor community post-Holi gatherings, but no traffic-disrupting public festivities are expected.

### **Resident Summary**
Today is a high-alert day for **resource management**. Prioritize water conservation and be prepared for potential food service disruptions at local eateries due to the LPG shortage. For travel, the Old Madras Road remains slow-moving; consider using the Purple Line Metro to bypass the Tin Factory junction where possible.
