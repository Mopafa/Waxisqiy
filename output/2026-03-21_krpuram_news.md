**KR Puram Local News Bulletin: Saturday, March 21, 2026**

### **1. Traffic & Transit Update**
*   **Tin Factory/Hanging Bridge:** Expect the usual high-volume congestion at the Tin Factory junction and the KR Puram Hanging Bridge. While the Purple Line metro is operational, ongoing construction for the **ORR-Airport Metro line** continues to cause narrowing of lanes near the Benniganahalli area.
*   **Citywide Advisory:** A major traffic advisory is in effect today for **Eid-ul-Fitr/Ramzan prayers**. Large congregations (15,000–20,000 people) are expected at B.B. Junction and Chamarajapete. While these are centered on Mysore Road, the resulting diversions and increased movement are likely to cause ripple effects on the Outer Ring Road (ORR) and Old Madras Road.
*   **Commute Tip:** Motorists heading toward Whitefield or Hebbal via Tin Factory should expect delays of 15–20 minutes during peak hours. Use the **Benniganahalli Metro Station** to bypass the surface-level bottleneck.

### **2. Local Civic Alerts**
*   **Power Supply:** BESCOM has reported **no scheduled power cuts** for the KR Puram zone today. However, residents are advised to keep the helpline (1912) handy for any local transformer issues or emergency repairs.
*   **Water Alerts:** No specific water supply disruptions have been announced by BWSSB for KR Puram today. 
*   **Infrastructure:** Pedestrians are reminded to use the automated signals at the mouth of the Hanging Bridge. Recent decongestion efforts have shifted certain BMTC bus stops away from the bridge mouth to the skywalk area (100m back) to prevent buses from blocking the main carriageway.

### **3. Holi 2026 Celebrations**
*   **Note on Date:** While the main Holi festival (Rangwali Holi) occurred on **March 4, 2026**, some extended celebrations are still active in the city.
*   **Ongoing Events:** The **"Phoolon Ki Holi"** (Flower Holi) featuring DJ Ravi continues at Nolimmits Lounge (central Bangalore, accessible via the Purple Line from KR Puram) and is scheduled to run through **March 25**.
