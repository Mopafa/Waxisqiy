**KR Puram Resident Bulletin | Saturday, March 7, 2026**

### **1. Traffic & Transit Alert: Tin Factory / Hanging Bridge**
The KR Puram–Tin Factory corridor remains a critical bottleneck today. Significant changes have been implemented to manage the congestion:
*   **One-Way Restriction:** A stretch of **Old Madras Road** (from BBMP Junction to the Veterinary Hospital near the KR Puram Law and Order Police Station) has been converted to a **one-way**.
*   **U-Turn Closure:** The U-turn at the **BBMP Junction** on Old Madras Road is now closed. Vehicles heading toward TC Palya or Anandapura must use the free left turn near the police station.
*   **Infrastructure Update:** Following the **Karnataka Budget 2026** announcement yesterday, **₹450 crore** has been officially allocated to transform the Outer Ring Road (ORR) stretch from Silk Board to KR Puram into a "Global Standard Corridor," which will involve upcoming lane upgrades and elevated structures.

### **2. Local Civic Alerts**
*   **BESCOM Power Cut:** KR Puram is among the areas scheduled for maintenance-related power outages today, **March 7**, and tomorrow, **March 8**. Expect temporary disruptions between **9:00 AM and 5:00 PM** (or 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM in some blocks) as BESCOM conducts essential transformer and feeder line servicing.
*   **BWSSB Water Notice:** KR Puram has been identified by the IISc and BWSSB as one of 65 wards at risk for groundwater depletion this summer. Residents are advised to use water judiciously. The Board is currently prioritizing new connections and monitoring borewells in the area to stabilize supply.
*   **Civic Governance:** The Greater Bengaluru Authority (GBA) has released the **Draft Electoral List 2026**. Residents are encouraged to verify their details at local ward offices ahead of the upcoming June 30 civic poll deadline.
