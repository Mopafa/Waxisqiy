### **1. Traffic Update: Tin Factory & Hanging Bridge**
*   **High Congestion:** Expect significant delays at the **Tin Factory junction** and the **KR Puram Hanging Bridge**. Major redevelopment work on the Outer Ring Road (ORR) corridor between Silk Board and KR Puram has led to narrowed carriageways, causing heavy bottlenecks.
