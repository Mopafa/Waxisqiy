### **KR Puram Local News Bulletin: Friday, April 3, 2026**

Today is **Good Friday**, a public holiday. While the city observes a long weekend, here is the essential live update for KR Puram and surrounding areas.

*   **Current Flow:** Traffic at the Tin Factory junction and Hanging Bridge is reported as **moderate** this morning due to the public holiday. However, a surge in outward-bound interstate buses (towards Kolar/Hoskote) is expected by late evening.
