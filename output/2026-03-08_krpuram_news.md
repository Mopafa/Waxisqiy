March 8, 2026 | **KR Puram Local Bulletin**

### **🚦 Traffic & Commute**
*   **Tin Factory/Hanging Bridge:** Expect persistent "gridlock" conditions. Ongoing Metro Phase 2 work and road widening continue to cause 15–20 minute delays during peak hours. 
*   **Nagawara Closure:** **Nagawara Main Road** remains completely closed for white-topping between Nagawara Junction and Govindapura Junction. This closure is expected to last until **March 22, 2026**. Commuters heading toward North Bangalore should use the Hebbal-Outer Ring Road bypass.
*   **Bus-Bay Alert:** The city bus stop at the mouth of the KR Puram Hanging Bridge remains shifted 100m back (below the skywalk) to ease the bottleneck at the bridge entry.

### **⚠️ Local Civic Alerts**
*   **9-Hour Power Shutdown:** BESCOM has scheduled a major maintenance shutdown today, **March 8, from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM**. KR Puram, along with Whitefield and Mahadevapura, is affected. Residents are advised to charge essential devices and store water early.
*   **Critical Water Alert:** As of March 7, KR Puram has been placed in the **"Red Zone"** for water stress. Authorities have warned of high borewell failure risks this summer. A penal ban is now in effect for using potable water for car washing or gardening; "Water Police" units are actively patrolling the East Corporation limits.
*   **Administrative Change:** Residents are reminded that under the new Greater Bengaluru Authority restructuring, KR Puram now falls under the **Bengaluru East City Corporation**.
