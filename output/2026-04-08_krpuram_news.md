**Local News Bulletin: KR Puram, Bangalore**
**Date:** April 8, 2026

### **1. Traffic Update: Tin Factory & Hanging Bridge**
*   **Congestion Warning:** Expect heavy slow-moving traffic at the Tin Factory junction and the KR Puram Hanging Bridge throughout the evening. Ongoing construction for the **Metro Blue Line (Airport line)** and Purple Line integration continues to cause bottlenecks.
*   **Rain Impact:** The **IMD has issued a Yellow Alert** for Bangalore today. Scattered thunderstorms and rain are expected this evening, which typically leads to severe waterlogging and gridlock in the Tin Factory area. Commuters are advised to finish travel by 5:00 PM to avoid the peak "rain-traffic" surge.
*   **Suggested Route:** Use the **Kasturi Nagar** or **Hennur** underpasses if heading toward Hebbal to bypass the main bridge snarls.

*   **Weather Alert:** A Yellow Alert is in effect for April 8. Residents should prepare for gusty winds (30–40 kmph) and lightning. Secure loose items on balconies and avoid parking under old trees or billboards.
