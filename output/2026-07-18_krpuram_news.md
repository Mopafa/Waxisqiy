### **KR Puram Local Bulletin: July 18, 2026**

#### **🚦 Traffic & Commute: Tin Factory & Hanging Bridge**
*   **Congestion Status:** Heavy traffic persists at the **Tin Factory junction** and the **KR Puram Hanging Bridge**. Commuters should expect significant delays during peak hours (8:30 AM – 11:30 AM and 5:30 PM – 9:00 PM) due to ongoing **Blue Line Metro** construction on the Outer Ring Road (ORR).
*   **Metro Updates:** BMRCL has confirmed that the **KR Puram–Silk Board** section is in its final phase of viaduct work. Trial runs are officially slated to begin in **October 2026**.
*   **Infrastructure Alert:** Residents are advised that the dedicated cycling lanes previously on the ORR have been removed to facilitate Metro pillar work. No new diversions have been announced for today, but the bottleneck near the KR Puram Railway Station remains a critical choke point.

#### **🏢 Local Civic Alerts**
*   **E-Khata Mandatory:** Effective **July 1, 2026**, the BBMP (Greater Bengaluru Authority) has made **Digital E-Khata** mandatory for all building plan approvals. Paper-based ownership records are no longer accepted for verification.
*   **Metro Penalty Hike:** Under the revised *Metro Railways Act 2026*, BMRCL has implemented stricter monetary penalties for minor offenses on premises (e.g., littering or obstructing doors), with fines now reaching up to **₹25,000**.
*   **Monsoon Preparedness:** BBMP has deployed additional "Pothole Task Forces" in the Mahadevpura zone following recent rains to address craters on the service roads near the Hanging Bridge.

#### **🎨 Holi 2026 Update**
*   **Clarification:** Please note that **Holi 2026 was celebrated on March 3–4, 2026**. There are no official Holi celebrations or "Festival of Colors" events scheduled for today, July 18.
*   **Today's Major Event:** For those seeking cultural activities today, the **Mahesh Kale Abhangwari** concert is taking place in Bengaluru this evening.

#### **📅 Resident Advisory**
*   **Travel Tip:** If traveling toward Whitefield or Hebbal, residents are encouraged to use the **Purple Line Metro** to bypass the Tin Factory surface traffic.
