**KR PURAM LOCAL BULLETIN | WEDNESDAY, JULY 1, 2026**

### **1. Traffic Update: Tin Factory & Hanging Bridge**
*   **Hanging Bridge:** Heavy congestion is reported at the entry point of the cable-stayed bridge (K.R. Puram bridge) from the Mahadevapura and Hoskote sides. The automated traffic signal at the Tin Factory end is active, but expect wait times of **15–20 minutes** during peak hours.
*   **Tin Factory Junction:** Motorists should anticipate severe bottlenecks due to ongoing metro work and long-distance buses stopping on the main road. 
*   **Underpass Alert:** While the Foot Over Bridge (FOB) at the KR Puram Metro station is open, heavy pedestrian crossing at-grade near the railway station continues to slow down traffic moving towards Kasturinagar.

### **2. Civic Alerts: Footpath Crackdown & Power/Water**
*   **Safe Footpath Campaign:** Starting **today (July 1)**, the BBMP and traffic police are launching a city-wide crackdown on footpath encroachments. 
    *   **Action:** Shop owners must remove all displays, signboards, and obstacles from footpaths immediately.
    *   **Vehicle Towing:** Any vehicle parked on a footpath will be towed.
    *   **Abandoned Vehicles:** Authorities will begin "stickering" abandoned vehicles today. Owners have until July 10 to move them before they are towed and auctioned.
*   **Water Supply:** BWSSB has announced a potential disruption in water supply today, **July 1**, in various eastern pockets due to essential repair work on the Thorekadanahalli pipeline. Residents are advised to use water judiciously.
*   **Gruha Jyothi Drive:** A state-wide door-to-door verification for the "Gruha Jyothi" free electricity scheme begins today; residents may see officials visiting for record updates.

### **3. Holi 2026 Status**
*   **Celebration Check:** Please note that **Holi 2026 was celebrated on March 4, 2026**. There are no Holi festivals or major "Festival of Colors" events scheduled for today, July 1. Local events today are focused on temple rituals, such as the *Panihati* observances at nearby ISKCON centers.

### **Resident Advice:**
Use the **Old Madras Road corridor** with caution during evening peaks. If traveling to Whitefield, consider using the **Purple Line Metro** to avoid the Tin Factory gridlock. Ensure your vehicles are not parked on public pavements to avoid fines during the new enforcement drive.
