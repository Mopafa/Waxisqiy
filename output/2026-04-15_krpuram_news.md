**BENGALURU LOCAL BULLETIN: KR PURAM**
**Date:** Wednesday, April 15, 2026
**Weather:** Expect typical summer heat with highs around 35°C; staying hydrated is advised as the city manages a seasonal water crunch.

### **1. Traffic & Transit: Tin Factory / Hanging Bridge**
*   **Persistent Congestion:** Traffic at the Tin Factory junction and the KR Puram Hanging Bridge remains heavy today. Commuters should account for an additional 20–30 minutes during peak hours due to ongoing **Blue Line Metro construction** (Silk Board to KR Puram), which is slated for completion in December 2026.
*   **Enforcement Alert:** Bengaluru Traffic Police (BTP) have launched a strict citywide crackdown on **triple-riding** on two-wheelers. Residents in KR Puram and surrounding areas like Mahadevapura are being monitored; violators face fines between ₹500 and ₹1,000.
*   **Route Reminder:** Buses continue to halt at the shifted stop below the skywalk (100m from the bridge mouth) to prevent gridlock on the main flyover.

### **2. Civic Alerts & Infrastructure**
*   **Water Supply:** The BWSSB has flagged a groundwater crisis in KR Puram. "Mini water tanks" have been deployed in high-density pockets to mitigate shortages. Residents are reminded that a **3% annual water tariff increase** came into effect on April 1.
*   **Pothole Repairs:** While the BBMP (now transitioning to the **Bengaluru East Corporation**) claims to have filled nearly 40,000 potholes citywide as of late March, local reports indicate several pending patches in KR Puram’s interior roads. Use the "Sahaaya" app to report new craters.
*   **Power:** BESCOM reports **no scheduled power outages** for KR Puram today, though localized maintenance may cause brief intermittent disruptions.
*   **Safety:** Local police are on high alert following a recent incident involving an assault on hotel staff in the area. Increased night patrols are active.

### **3. Festivals & Celebrations**
*   **Holi 2026:** Please note that **Holi was celebrated on March 3–4, 2026**. No major Holi events are scheduled for today.
*   **Today’s Festivities:** Today marks **Poila Baisakh (Bengali New Year)** and follows yesterday's **Vishu** and **Puthandu** celebrations. 
    *   **Events:** Look for "Baisakhi Dhamaka" Punjabi nights and special Bengali New Year "Banglar Bhoj" feasts at major hotels along the Outer Ring Road (ORR) and in nearby Indiranagar.
    *   **Temple Visits:** Local temples in KR Puram are expecting higher footfall today for New Year prayers; expect minor localized traffic near temple entrances.

**Resident Tip:** Use the Metro for travel toward Whitefield or MG Road to bypass the Tin Factory bottleneck during the evening rush.
