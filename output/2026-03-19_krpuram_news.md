**KR PURAM LOCAL BULLETIN: March 19, 2026**

### **1. Traffic & Transit: Tin Factory / Hanging Bridge**
*   **Festive Rush:** Heavy congestion is reported at **Tin Factory Junction** and the **KR Puram Hanging Bridge** today due to the **Ugadi (Karnataka New Year)** festival. 
*   **Private Bus Violations:** Authorities have issued warnings regarding private buses at the Tin Factory bus stand. Reports indicate severe overcrowding and passengers traveling on rooftops to reach hometowns (Chintamani, Madanapalle, Tirupati).
*   **Police Drive:** KR Puram Traffic Police are on high alert following a special drive yesterday that booked 22 riders for "wheeling" stunts near Old Madras Road and Battarahalli Junction.
*   **Ongoing Works:** Commuters should expect slow-moving traffic towards Mahadevapura and Whitefield due to persistent Metro construction bottlenecks near the Benniganahalli station area.

### **2. Local Civic Alerts**
*   **Water Crisis & Penalties:** BWSSB has implemented a strict ban on using potable (Cauvery/borewell) water for non-essential purposes, including car washing, gardening, and construction. Violators face a **₹5,000 fine**.
*   **Water-Stressed Zones:** KR Puram and surrounding pockets in East Bengaluru are among the **448 high-alert water-stressed zones** identified by BWSSB. Residents are advised to use treated water for non-drinking needs.
*   **Stormwater Drain Audit:** A recent citizen audit of the K200 drain highlights potential flooding risks in low-lying residential layouts nearby; residents are urged to report any blockages to the BBMP control room.

### **3. Festival Update: Holi vs. Ugadi**
*   **Ugadi (Today):** Today, **March 19, 2026**, is the primary festival being celebrated in KR Puram. Expect local temples to be crowded for morning rituals (*Panchanga Sravanam*).
*   **Holi 2026:** While the main Holi celebrations (Rangwali Holi) took place on **March 4, 2026**, some extended events are still active:
    *   **Phoolon Ki Holi:** Ongoing at *Nolimmits Lounge and Club* (until March 25).
    *   **Belated Celebrations:** Most large-scale open-air parties in KR Puram (like the ones at Bella House Go Go) concluded earlier this month. Residents looking for "colors" today will find the focus has shifted to traditional Ugadi festivities.
