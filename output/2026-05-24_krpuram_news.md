**Local News Bulletin: KR Puram, Bengaluru**
**Date:** Sunday, May 24, 2026

### **1. Traffic Update: Tin Factory & Hanging Bridge**
*   **Congestion Alert:** Expect heavy traffic and slow-moving vehicles at the **Tin Factory Junction** and the **KR Puram Hanging Bridge**. This is due to a combination of the ongoing **Outer Ring Road (ORR) redevelopment project** (Silk Board to KR Puram stretch) and the current **Yellow Alert** for rain in the city.
*   **Key Bottlenecks:** The entry point to the bridge near the HP fuel station and the bottleneck under the bridge near the railway station remain high-congestion zones.
*   **Advisory:** Commuters are advised to use the **Metro Purple Line** (Challaghatta-Whitefield) to bypass the Tin Factory gridlock. If driving, consider alternative routes such as the IOC-Mukunda Theatre road or Lingarajapuram flyover.

### **2. Civic Alerts**
*   **Weather Warning:** The IMD has issued a **Yellow Alert** for Bengaluru today. Heavy rainfall, thunderstorms, and gusty winds are expected. Residents in low-lying areas of KR Puram and Mahadevapura should be wary of potential waterlogging.
*   **Power Supply:** **BESCOM** has no scheduled power cuts for today; however, emergency outages are possible due to rain-related tree falls or transformer issues. You can report outages via the helpline **1912**.
*   **Water Supply:** No major maintenance shutdowns are reported for the KR Puram zone today. However, residents are encouraged to store water as a precaution against localized pipe repairs often triggered by heavy rains.
