### **KR Puram Local News Bulletin: Sunday, April 5, 2026**

**Today's Outlook:** Residents should prepare for a busy Sunday, primarily driven by a high-profile IPL match in the city and ongoing summer civic challenges.

---

### **1. Traffic & Transit: Tin Factory & Hanging Bridge**
*   **Persistent Bottlenecks:** Expect heavy congestion at the **Tin Factory Junction** and the **KR Puram Hanging Bridge** throughout the day. While recent barricading of the bus stop at the bridge mouth has slightly improved flow, the "chicken neck" near the railway station remains a primary delay point.
*   **IPL Surge Advisory:** A significant increase in traffic is expected on **Old Madras Road** and the **Outer Ring Road** starting from 4:00 PM as fans head toward the M. Chinnaswamy Stadium for the **RCB vs. CSK** match (7:30 PM). 
*   **Commuter Tip:** Avoid the KR Puram to Hoskote stretch if possible; travel times to the Chennai Expressway entry are currently averaging 60–90 minutes during peak hours.

### **2. Local Civic Alerts & Utilities**
*   **Water Scarcity Alert:** KR Puram and Ramamurthy Nagar have been identified by the BWSSB as **"water-stressed" wards** for Summer 2026. Residents are urged to use Cauvery water strictly for domestic needs and avoid using it for car washing or gardening to escape heavy fines.
*   **Power Supply:** BESCOM has reported **no scheduled power shutdowns** for the KR Puram division today. However, localized "emergency maintenance" may occur due to high summer load.
*   **Groundwater Crisis:** Borewell yields in the neighborhood continue to drop; many residents are now reporting the need to deepen existing wells beyond 1,500 feet.

### **3. Festivals & Events: Clarification on Holi 2026**
*   **Holi Update:** Please note that **Holi 2026 was celebrated on March 3rd**. There are no major Holi-themed public events scheduled for today, April 5.
*   **Easter Sunday:** Today marks **Easter Sunday**. Local churches in KR Puram and Devasandra are holding special morning services; expect minor localized traffic near religious centers until noon.
*   **Major Event:** The biggest event in the city today is the **IPL 2026 Match 11: Royal Challengers Bengaluru vs. Chennai Super Kings**. Expect a festive, "stadium-like" atmosphere in local sports bars and community hubs throughout the evening.

### **4. Weather & Health**
*   **Summer Heat:** Temperatures are expected to peak at **36°C**. Residents are advised to stay hydrated, especially those attending open-air events or local markets.
*   **Civic Protest:** Minor "Scream" protests by citizen groups regarding infrastructure delays (part of the Bengaluru Rising campaign) may occur near major flyovers; keep an eye on local ward groups for real-time updates.
