**Live Local Bulletin: KR Puram, Bengaluru – Friday, March 20, 2026**

### **1. Traffic Update: Tin Factory & Hanging Bridge**
*   **Current Status:** Expect significant congestion at the **Tin Factory junction** and the **KR Puram Hanging Bridge** today. Heavy traffic is being reported due to the first spell of **pre-monsoon showers and localized hailstorms** across Bengaluru this afternoon, causing slippery roads and water-logging in low-lying areas.
*   **Bottlenecks:** The usual "chaos" persists at the mouth of the bridge near the HP fuel station, exacerbated by BMTC buses stopping at non-designated spots.
*   **Alert:** A city-wide traffic advisory has been issued for **tomorrow, March 21**, due to large-scale **Eid prayers** (15,000–20,000 people expected at various junctions). While primary closures are on Mysore Road and Chamarajapete, expect increased traffic movement in KR Puram today as residents prepare for the festivities.

### **2. Local Civic Alerts**
*   **Water Crisis High Alert:** BWSSB has identified **KR Puram** as one of the 448 "high-alert" water-stressed zones for Summer 2026. To mitigate the shortage, the board is deploying **mini-water tanks** (5,000-liter capacity) in interior stretches of KR Puram and Mahadevapura. Residents are urged to use potable water only for essential purposes.
*   **BBMP Restructuring:** As part of the **Greater Bengaluru Governance Act**, KR Puram is now officially being transitioned into the newly formed **Bengaluru East Corporation**. Final voter lists were published earlier this week (March 16) in preparation for the upcoming civic polls.
*   **LPG Shortage:** A temporary LPG supply crunch is affecting local households; the state government has requested a one-week wait for normalcy to return.

### **3. Holi & Festive Celebrations**
