### **KR Puram Local Bulletin: April 20, 2026**

#### **1. Major Festival Alert: Basava Jayanti & Akshaya Tritiya**
*   **Basava Jayanti:** Today is a **State General Holiday** in Karnataka. Expect local parades and floral tributes at Basaveshwara statues in and around KR Puram. 
*   **Akshaya Tritiya:** The auspicious period for gold purchases and new beginnings concludes today (the *Tithi* ends at approximately 7:27 AM). High footfall is expected at jewelry stores in the KR Puram and Phoenix Marketcity vicinity.
*   **Holi 2026:** Residents are reminded that Holi was celebrated on **March 4, 2026**. There are no official Holi events scheduled for today.

#### **2. Traffic & Transit: Tin Factory / Hanging Bridge**
*   **Holiday Impact:** Traffic flow at the **Tin Factory junction** and the **KR Puram Hanging Bridge** is expected to be lighter than a typical Monday due to the public holiday. However, intermittent delays may occur near temples and community centers due to Basava Jayanti processions.
*   **Big Infrastructure News:** The Karnataka government has officially announced a new **28-km elevated corridor** connecting **Yeshwanthpur to KR Puram**. This project aims to bypass the chronic bottlenecks at Tin Factory and Hebbal, with tenders expected to be invited shortly.
*   **Metro Update:** Work continues on the Blue Line (Silk Board to KR Puram), which remains on track for its late-2026 completion goal.

#### **3. Civic Alerts**
*   **Power Supply:** According to the latest BESCOM update for April 20, **no major scheduled power cuts** are planned for the KR Puram zone today. Minor localized maintenance may still occur; residents can call 1912 for specific queries.
*   **Water Scarcity:** KR Puram has been identified as a **high-alert water-stressed zone** this summer. The BWSSB is deploying mini-water tanks (Sintex) to interior clusters to act as drinking water buffers. Residents are urged to use water judiciously as groundwater extraction in Bengaluru East has reached critical levels.
*   **Tariff Change:** A reminder that a **3% annual hike** in water bills became effective on April 1.

#### **4. Local News Spotlight**
*   **Tragic Incident:** Local news reports today confirm a tragic drowning incident in the Cauvery river (Mysuru district) involving residents from **SBR Layout, KR Puram**. The family was attending an *Urus* gathering on Sunday when the accident occurred.

**Resident Tip:** Since today is a public holiday, government offices and many private banks will remain closed. Plan your errands accordingly!
