**Local News Bulletin: KR Puram, Bangalore**
**Date:** May 16, 2026

### **1. Traffic Update: Tin Factory & Hanging Bridge**
*   **Current Status:** Expect significant delays at the **Tin Factory junction** and the **KR Puram Hanging Bridge** today. Heavy congestion is reported on the Old Madras Road stretch as the city prepares for predicted pre-monsoon showers this evening.
*   **Bottlenecks:** The transition from the ORR flyover towards Hebbal remains a "choke point." Traffic police recommend using the **Benniganahalli Metro Station** skywalk area for bus boarding to avoid the dangerous foot-traffic surge at the mouth of the bridge.
*   **Alert:** With rainfall expected today, the "snail-pace" traffic typical of this zone is likely to worsen during peak evening hours (5:00 PM – 8:30 PM).

### **2. Civic Alerts & Local News**
*   **Power Supply:** **BESCOM** has warned of potential "unscheduled outages" today due to expected rain and wind, which often lead to feeder trips in the KR Puram and Horamavu subdivisions. Residents can report issues via the **ASTraM app** or by calling **1912**.

No scheduled water cuts are reported for today, but residents are urged to ensure Rainwater Harvesting (RWH) systems are ready to avoid penalties starting next month.
*   **Crime Alert:** Local police reported the arrest of two individuals today for a knifepoint robbery in the KR Puram area. Vigilance is advised in less-crowded lanes during late hours.
