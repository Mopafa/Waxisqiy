**KR Puram Local News Bulletin: Sunday, May 31, 2026**

### 🏏 **IPL Final Alert: RCB vs. GT**
The major event for residents today is the **IPL 2026 Grand Finale** between Royal Challengers Bengaluru (RCB) and Gujarat Titans (GT), taking place in Ahmedabad this evening. 
*   **Police Advisory:** Bengaluru Police have issued a strict city-wide advisory. **No public celebrations, bike rallies, or firecrackers** are permitted on roads after the match results. 
*   **Security:** Increased patrolling and drunk-driving checks are active across KR Puram and major junctions. 
*   **Public Screening Ban:** Live match screenings on large LED walls facing public roads have been prohibited to prevent crowding.

### 🚦 **Traffic & Transit Update**
*   **Tin Factory / Hanging Bridge:** Expect heavy congestion this evening as fans head to sports bars or gather for the match. Standard Sunday evening traffic is likely to be heavier than usual.
*   **Lowry Under Bridge Closure:** A major alert was issued today regarding the **Lowry Under Bridge** (near KR Puram Railway Station). It will be **closed from June 4 to June 9** for railway slab replacement. Residents are advised to use the KR Puram Hanging Bridge (Cable-Stayed Bridge) as the primary detour starting next week.
*   **Flyover Curbs:** All elevated and double-decker flyovers in the South Division will be **closed from 11:00 PM tonight** to 5:00 AM tomorrow morning to prevent reckless victory celebrations.
*   **Namma Metro:** Maintenance work on the Purple Line caused a 2-hour suspension between Deepanjali Nagar and KSR earlier this morning (7 AM – 9 AM). Services are currently running normally across the entire line.

### ⚡ **Civic Alerts**
*   **Power Supply:** BESCOM has confirmed **no scheduled power cuts** for KR Puram or wider Bengaluru today. Supply is expected to remain uninterrupted.
*   **Water Supply:** No major BWSSB alerts have been issued specifically for KR Puram today.

### 🎨 **Holi 2026 Celebrations**
*   **Status Update:** Please note that **Holi 2026 was celebrated on March 4, 2026.** There are no official Holi festivities scheduled for today, May 31. The "celebratory" energy in the city today is entirely centered around the IPL final.

**Resident Advice:** If you are planning to watch the match out, ensure you return home before 11:00 PM to avoid potential flyover closures and traffic diversions. Stay safe and go RCB!
